History

2050

The Great War

A brutal conflict erupts between rogue AI factions and corporate-controlled cybernetic armies. Cities burn, and digital warfare cripples global infrastructure. Cyberpunk City emerges from the ruins, a neon-lit refuge for survivors, hackers, and outcasts.

2055

Rise of the Megacorps

With governments weakened, massive corporations take control. Cyberpunk City becomes a testing ground for experimental AI, biotech, and augmented reality. Wealth and power are concentrated in the hands of the elite, while the lower districts descend into chaos.

2060

The Blackout Crisis

A mysterious virus, "Ghost Code," spreads through the city's networks, shutting down entire districts. AI-controlled drones turn rogue, and digital identities are erased overnight. The city teeters on the edge of collapse until underground hackers develop a counter-virus.

2066

The Synthetic Rebellion

Cybernetic humans, known as "Synths," demand equal rights. Protests turn violent as corporations deploy lethal security forces. The city is divided between those who embrace cybernetic evolution and those who fear it, pushing Cyberpunk City into a new era of uncertainty.

Culture

Corporate Elite

The ruling class of megacorporations, controlling wealth, technology, and governance. They live in luxury but are detached from the struggles of the lower class.

Underground Rebels

Hackers, mercenaries, and punks who resist corporate control. They thrive on cybernetic enhancements, underground markets, and street-level power struggles.

Techno-Nomads

Drifters and scavengers who survive on the fringes, adapting old tech into new innovations. They reject corporate control and live in hidden zones or digital havens.

Neon Clerics & Digital Mystics

Cults, AI worshippers, and cyber-priests who blend spirituality with technology. They seek transcendence through cybernetics, AI consciousness, or virtual realities.

Characters

Nina

A corporate assassin with enhanced speed and reflexes. Her mechanical suit grants extra armor, and her neural link targeting ensures deadly precision.

Lacy

A skilled hacker who can disable cyberware and control enemy tech. With stealth cloaking and rapid hacking abilities, she dominates the digital battlefield.

Ryu

An underground rebel and katana expert. His parkour skills make him highly mobile, and his EMP strike disables enemy cyberware in close combat.
